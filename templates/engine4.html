💡 What do these flag types mean?
COGS_DRIFT — Cost of Goods Sold in your sales records differs from what was booked in the General Ledger by more than 5%. This means costs may have been recorded incorrectly.
MARGIN_ALERT — Profit margin dropped below 15% in this period. Could mean prices are too low, costs too high, or discounts are eroding profits.
LOGISTICS_SPIKE — Shipping and delivery costs jumped by more than $5,000 compared to GL in this period. Possible overcharging or missed accruals.
MARKETING_OVERSPEND — Marketing spend exceeded 15% of gross revenue. The campaign may not be generating enough return.
